# Twilight

Twilight is Sarah Florence, a necromancer whose powers cause her to develop psychosis and have psychotic breaks. The cause of her break at the end of Majo's solo series is her giving a pie to Majo and Majo taking it. Sarah and Majo are also exes; they were in a polyamorous relationship with Abigail Rodriguez before her death in the Crash. While Lectric was trying to seduce Rock Woman, Sarah was torturing Majo alongside Dangerous, using the likeness of Abigail Rodriguez and Dangerous's mind control to distort her perception of reality. Majo is only saved by Silenced Wren saying something out loud for the first time since the Crash.
