# Dangerous

Dangerous is Diana Rice, an actress who was in Michelle MacDuffie's car during the Crash. She has mind control powers; the side effects turn off her inhibitions and cause her to want her desires to be fulfilled. She kidnaps Xscape and forces him to satisfy her every desire and lust. Privacy and Wren worked together to free Jackson from her clutches. She eventually teams up with Twilight to torture Majo Majima by altering Majo's perception of reality.
