# Majo

Majo Majima is the Witch of Therapy. She heals people; her powers seemingly have no side effects. She's in therapy because of her depression and guilt, mostly due to her wishing she could have saved her girlfriend at the time of the Crash. She joins Therapy with the MacDuffies to fight Dangerous and Twilight. Her solo run ends with her losing her eye after thinking Sarah was rehabilitated. She's Rock Woman's fiancée and eventual wife. She went by the name Sympathy at one point before dropping it.
