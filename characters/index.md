# Characters

Crash survivors' codenames are drawn from song titles, with the source artist determined by which car they were in: survivors of Car 1 (Michelle MacDuffie's car) take Michael Jackson track titles, with the exception of Silenced Wren; survivors of Car 2 (Majo's car) take Metric track titles. Lectric takes a Film School track title. Threshold takes a Sex Bob-omb track title. Hardwire takes a Metric track title, as her car was traveling northbound with Car 2.

## Justice Alliance

- [Arrowhead](Arrowhead.md) — Jo Youngman, a genderfluid 27-year-old competitive archer with no powers, just specialty arrows and the most accurate eye in any room. Aroace and settled in their identity, in therapy mostly about everyone else's reaction to it.
- [Duckman](Duckman.md) — Andrew Wolfe, billionaire heir turned vigilante protector of Greenville. No powers — just detective skills, a glide suit, and a grief so industrious he's still not sure if it counts as healing.
- [Mosquito Man]('Mosquito Man.md') — Mathew Whitney, 15, bitten by an enchanted mosquito and gifted with flight and wall-crawling. Built fake wings as misdirection and a sonic disarmament buzzer. Street-level hero in Detroit; his parents think he's in robotics club.
- [Red Lightning]('Red Lightning.md') — A mechanically enhanced speedster from 2170 who ran too fast and ended up in the present. Protects Detroit while living with his future ancestors and building the suit that keeps his speed from killing him.
- [Rock Woman]('Rock Woman.md') — Alice Brown, an ancient being of uncertain age in a woman's form. Telekinesis, precognition, immortality, and memories that work in reverse — the distant past is sharp; last Tuesday is gone. Engaged to Majo.

## Therapy

- [Majo](Majo.md) — Majo Majima, healer whose powers couldn't save Abigail Rodriguez in the Crash. Carries survivor's guilt and depression. Survived a sustained villain campaign and lost her eye to a betrayal she still wishes hadn't been one. Engaged to Rock Woman.
- [Privacy](Privacy.md) — Michelle MacDuffie, famous musician with invisibility and silencing forcefields. Developed severe clinical paranoia after the Crash. In therapy for her own deterioration, her son Jackson's trauma, and the guilt she carries toward Trinity.
- [Silenced Wren](Silenced Wren.md) — Trinity May Grace, 16 at the Crash, vibration control powers and trauma-based mutism. Has not spoken since the Crash — until she said Abigail's name out loud to free Majo. In Therapy reluctantly; present, but not yet forgiving.
- [Xscape](Xscape.md) — Jackson MacDuffie, 17, teleporter who sees alternate realities as a constant perceptual overlay. Leaves X-shaped burn marks under stress. Was held in servitude by Dangerous as a teenager. Still learning what it means to choose.

## Villains

- [Dangerous](Dangerous.md) — Diana Rice, actress with mind control powers and no remaining inhibitions. Kidnapped Jackson MacDuffie, orchestrated the campaign against Majo, and is still at large. Has declined therapy.
- [Lectric](Lectric.md) — Alexander Randall, electrical powers with a compulsive greed side effect. Infiltrated Therapy as a fake ally, ran a black widow scheme targeting Rock Woman, and fed intelligence to the other villains. Now openly aligned with the enemy.
- [Twilight](Twilight.md) — Sarah Florence, necromancer whose every use of her powers deepens her psychosis. Was in a relationship with Majo and Abigail before the Crash. Her grief for Abigail has been weaponized. Cost Majo her eye under a pretense of reconciliation. At large.

## Other Characters

- [Abigail Rodriguez]('Abigail Rodriguez.md') — 24 years old, died in the Crash. Environmental science grad student and the emotional center of her relationship with Majo and Sarah. The only fatality, and the absence that drives the whole story.
- [Dr. Petra Okafor](Dr. Petra Okafor.md) — 48-year-old Nigerian-American trauma therapist. Runs individual and group sessions for Therapy members at the Meridian Center. Warm, precise, refuses easy comfort, and has her own therapist.
- [Hardwire](Hardwire.md) — Nadia Vasquez, 31, construction site manager whose car was caught in the Crash while traveling northbound. Can interface with and override electrical systems; powers surge involuntarily with strong emotions. Not affiliated with any team. Visits the Crash Site.
- [Imara Solis](Imara%20Solis.md) — 41-year-old textile engineer and former competitive judoka, founder of Quantum Fusion. Designs and builds Therapy's field costumes by hand. Was not in the Crash, has no powers, and knows more about how each team member's abilities work than most of their teammates do. Has declined therapy. The referral remains open.
- [Threshold](Threshold.md) — Marcus Webb, 34, paramedic first responder exposed at the Crash. Opens precise spatial portals. Has simultaneous claustrophobia and agoraphobia. Still works as a paramedic. Undeclared, watching.
