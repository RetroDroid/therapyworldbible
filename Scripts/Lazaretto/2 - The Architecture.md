# THE ARCHITECTURE
### A Lazaretto Script — No. 2

*Six weeks after the Crash. The electricity is real. He wants to be clear about that, in his own internal accounting: the electricity is real and it is his, and the question of when it became this precise is a question he has elected not to dwell on. The electricity presents. What the compound may actually have amplified is something he has described to no one.*
