# WARD L
### A Lazaretto Script — No. 1

*Seventeen months into five years. The room they gave him has been cleared — not cleaned. A cleaned room has its objects returned to their places. A cleared room has had its objects decided against. There is a distinction, and it matters to him, and he is learning to hold many distinctions precisely because in the absence of anything else, precision is what you have.*

The basin: the pressure behind the valve, the reach and temperature of the water if released at full force. The cot bolts: the torque required, the trajectory of the frame if freed. He reads these things the way you read ambient light. He does not do anything with them.*

*CAPTION: He has learned not to do anything with them. That is the primary skill the last seventeen months have taught him.*

## PAGE FOURTEEN (FULL PAGE)

*Wide shot: Reuben Marsh, seventeen months into five years, on a cot in a cleared room in a facility that is not a hospital and not billed as a hospital and that the paperwork calls a treatment program. The orange emergency light. The sealed window. The ventilation grille. The bolted cot. A man in the place designed to contain what he carries.*

*CAPTION: He does not yet know it will come back. But it will. Not on any schedule he can predict. Not in any way he will be able to plan for. It will return to this room, and he will learn to sit still enough to be present when it does, and the sitting still will eventually be the most practiced thing about him.*

*CAPTION: He does not sleep. He lies still and attends to the fact that something was here.*

*CAPTION: He is not wrong about what was here. He will build from this carefully, from the evidence of what keeps returning across choices and years, toward an understanding that will not be fully formed for a long time. He will be wrong about several of its features and will not be wrong about the thing itself. He will hold it precisely, privately, because the available evidence supports it and not because he needs it to be true.*

*CAPTION: He is seventeen months into five years. He has three years and seven months remaining. He has, in a way he does not yet have the word for, been found.*

*CAPTION: He lies still. He attends. He has learned — just now, tonight, in this specific hour — that this is the posture that is met.*
