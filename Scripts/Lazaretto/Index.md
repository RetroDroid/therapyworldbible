# Lazaretto (A Mini Series)
---
Lazaretto is a short, disjointed series of comics about Lazaretto, Lectric, The Snare, and Poe. 

[[1 - Ward L]] Lazaretto first perceives Poe, she does not appear to him, however.
[[2 - The Architecture]] Lectric first hits the snare.  His normal keen senses seem to have been enhanced by the QSL.
[[3 - The Name]] Lazaretto reads his evidence, reminding him who he's fighting for.
[[4 - The Darker Hands]] Lectric and Lazaretto both meet the Snare, and the Snare attempts to kill Lazaretto, but is prevented from doing so by Poe. This ends with a meeting of Lectric and Lazaretto, facilitated unwittingly by Threshold.
