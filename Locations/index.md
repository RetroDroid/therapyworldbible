# Locations

## Detroit

- [[Detroit]] — Home to both Red Lightning and Mosquito Man, who operate at different scales with an unspoken division of labor neither has formally acknowledged. A pragmatic city that regards its heroes with affectionate skepticism.

## Greenville

- [[Greenville]] — A mid-sized Midwestern city with historic bones and chronically underfunded services, protected by Duckman because it's the kind of city heroes fly over. Andrew Wolfe quietly funds its shelters, infrastructure repairs, and legal defense clinics.

## Justice Alliance

- [[Alliance Head Quarters]] — A functional, undecorated underground facility beneath Greenville's Waterfowl Migration Museum, shared by all five Alliance members. Built for briefings; mostly used for informal work sessions and the occasional quiet hour.
- [[The Gander]]  — Andrew Wolfe's cloaked aerial fortress hovering above Greenville, equipped with a forensic lab, comms array, medical bay, and training space. Built for a team. Usually occupied by one.

## Therapy

- [[Crash Site]] — The stretch of highway where the Crash happened, never quite returned to normal. Residual Quantum Spin Liquid produces electromagnetic interference, perceptual distortions, and temporal echoes of the night itself. Survivors visit. There are no answers here, only proximity.
- [[Meridian Center]] — Dr. Okafor's private, unlisted therapy practice on the third floor of an unremarkable building. Eight chairs in a circle for group sessions. The space where Therapy the team does the work that makes Therapy the team possible.
