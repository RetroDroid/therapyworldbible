# Greenville

Greenville is a mid-sized city in the American Midwest, best known for its river district, its historic architecture, and its persistently underfunded public services. It has the bones of a prosperous city and the budget of one that was promised a revival that never quite came.

Crime in Greenville is neither as dramatic nor as systemic as in Detroit. It's quieter — organized crime with institutional roots, petty violence that the local police handle with varying levels of competence, and occasional larger incidents that the Greenville PD is not equipped for and knows it. Duckman (Andrew Wolfe) chose to protect Greenville specifically because it is the kind of city heroes fly over on the way to somewhere more important. He is stubborn about this.

The city's relationship with Duckman is ambivalent. Most residents know something protects them — there are too many quiet resolutions to incidents that should have escalated, too many crime figures who turned themselves in with a suspiciously unified story about being scared. But nobody has a clear picture of who or what Duckman is. The Greenville Gazette ran a piece speculating he was a vigilante funded by Andrew Wolfe. They weren't wrong but couldn't prove it. The piece ran once and was not followed up on. The reporter has no memory of being spoken to about this.

Andrew Wolfe funds Greenville's largest homeless shelter and recreation center through his family foundation. The shelter is named after his parents. He does not discuss it publicly. He has also quietly funded infrastructure repairs in three neighborhoods, a youth archery program, and a legal defense clinic that has been operating for six years without anyone noticing who pays for it.

The river district contains the replica museum — a small institution dedicated to the history of waterfowl migration in the region — which serves as the concealed entrance to the Gander's ground-level lift. It is Andrew Wolfe's least subtle joke about himself. The museum gets around two hundred visitors a year. None of them have found the lift.
